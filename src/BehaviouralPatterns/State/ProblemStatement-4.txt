STATE PATTERN — FULL WALKTHROUGH (BAD → GOOD)
Problem Statement (Read Carefully)

You are building a Media Player.

The player has the following states:

Stopped
Playing
Paused

The player supports these operations:
play()
pause()
stop()


Rules
Current State	    play()	             pause()	        stop()
Stopped	           ▶️ → Playing	        ❌ Illegal	        ❌ Illegal
Playing	           ❌ Illegal	        ⏸ → Paused	        ⏹ → Stopped
Paused	           ▶️ → Playing	        ❌ Illegal	        ⏹ → Stopped

Illegal operations should throw IllegalStateException.
